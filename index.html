Who We Are

Florida Restoration Solutions, led by Leonor Uriarte and Silvia E. Smith, is based in Fruitland Park, Florida. We specialize in painting, bathroom remodeling, driveway and paver installation, garden decoration, and professional cleaning services.

With more than 5 years of experience in the industry, we combine creativity, efficiency, and professionalism to deliver reliable and high-quality restoration and home improvement services that enhance comfort, aesthetics, and long-term value for our clients.

At Florida Restoration Solutions, we are dedicated to improving and maintaining homes with services that combine creativity, efficiency, and professionalism. Our team offers complete solutions that cover painting, remodeling, landscaping accents, and cleaning, always tailored to the unique needs of our clients. We pride ourselves on being trustworthy, detail-oriented, and committed to results that exceed expectations.

Our Mission

To deliver reliable and high-quality restoration and home improvement services that enhance comfort, aesthetics, and long-term value for our clients.

Our Vision

To be recognized in Florida as a trusted leader in painting, remodeling, and home solutions, known for innovation, excellence, and personalized customer care.

Our Commitment to Safety

We prioritize safety for our clients, their properties, and our team throughout every project. From preparation to completion, we ensure secure, organized, and careful practices, creating a hazard-free environment that builds trust and peace of mind.
